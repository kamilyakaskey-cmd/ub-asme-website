We Win Every Year

UB ASME's Solar Splash team has an unbroken record of winning at the international Solar Splash competition. Year after year, our team proves that engineering excellence at Buffalo is second to none.

What is Solar Splash?

Solar Splash is the World Championship of intercollegiate solar boating, organized by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). Teams from universities across the country and around the world design, build, and race solar-powered watercraft in a series of events including sprint races, endurance challenges, and technical presentations.

Each team is responsible for every aspect of the boat — from the hull design and solar panel configuration to the motor and electrical systems. It's a true test of multidisciplinary engineering.
