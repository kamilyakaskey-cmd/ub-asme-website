Large Format 3D Printers

Our lab houses three 3D printers, each with different strengths. Together they cover a wide range of materials and part sizes — allowing our teams to print nearly every component in-house.

Snapmaker A350

Our largest printer. The A350's build volume allows us to produce large rover components as single parts, reducing assembly complexity. Provides stable performance, making it ideal for larger structural components where size matters.

Creality Ender 5 S1

Our most material-versatile printer. The Ender 5 S1 is the only printer in our lab capable of printing PET-GF (glass fiber reinforced), making it extremely valuable for chassis design where strength and stiffness are critical. Its build volume also allows larger rover components to be printed as single parts.

Build Volume
220 × 220 × 280 mm
Materials
PLA, PETG, TPU, PET-GF
Best For
Chassis & structural parts
Unique Capability
PET-GF (glass fiber)

Prusa MK3

Our most reliable printer for precision parts. The Prusa MK3's build volume is suited for small to medium-sized components, and its consistent, accurate print quality makes it the go-to for parts where tolerances matter. Supports both rigid and flexible materials.
