Engineering Week

Every February, the engineering clubs at the University at Buffalo come together to compete in Engineering Week — a week-long series of events where clubs go head to head for the glory of being the ultimate Engineering club at UB.

The final and biggest event of the week is the Battle Bots competition. Every engineering club participates, and the energy in the room is electric. It's an incredibly exciting event — even more so when you're in the arena building and fighting your own robot.

This year, UB ASME is building an entirely new and upgraded bot from the ground up — a bigger, better machine designed to dominate the competition once again.
