University at Buffalo — ASME Student Chapter

UB ASME

We are the University at Buffalo's chapter of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers — connecting students with hands-on engineering projects, competitions, and a community of future engineers.

Join Us Our Teams

What we build

Our Project Teams

Compete, design, and build alongside fellow engineers across five disciplines.

IAM3D

Additive manufacturing and 3D design — pushing the limits of what you can print.

Solar Splash

Design and race a solar-powered boat in an international collegiate competition.

e-HPVC

Engineer an electric human-powered vehicle for the ASME HPVC challenge.

Battle Bots

Build and battle combat robots — mechanical warfare, engineering style.

 Coming next semester

SDC

Self-Driving Car challenge — autonomous vehicle design coming soon to UB ASME.

More than a club

UB ASME gives engineers the chance to get their hands dirty. Whether you're into fabrication, electronics, software, or design — there's a team and a project for you.

We hold regular meetings, workshops, and events throughout the year. All UB students are welcome — no experience required.

Why Sponsor Us?

UB ASME fields five competitive engineering teams that design and build real systems — solar boats, combat robots, autonomous vehicles, human-powered vehicles, and 3D-printed machines. Every year, our teams compete nationally and internationally.

Sponsoring UB ASME means your brand reaches highly motivated engineering students, future industry professionals, and the University at Buffalo's engineering community. You'll be directly funding hands-on engineering education and student innovation.
