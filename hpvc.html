What is e-HPVC?

The ASME e-Human Powered Vehicle Challenge (e-HPVC) is a collegiate engineering competition where teams design, build, and race a fully enclosed electric-assist recumbent vehicle. The goal is to create a practical, sustainable, and efficient means of transportation — human-powered with electric assist — that could serve as a real-world alternative to cars for short-distance commuting.

Teams are judged on multiple criteria including the technical design report, the vehicle's innovation and practicality, and performance in both sprint and endurance races. It's one of the most complete engineering challenges in collegiate competition, requiring expertise across mechanical design, structural analysis, aerodynamics, and electrical systems.
