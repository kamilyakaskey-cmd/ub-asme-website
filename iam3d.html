2026 Rover — Florida EFX

Our 2026 project was a fully 3D-printed rover, competed at the Florida Engineering Fabrication Expo (EFX) hosted at the University of Central Florida (UCF).

Competition Highlights

The team traveled to Orlando, Florida to compete at Florida EFX at UCF — one of the premier collegiate fabrication competitions in the country.

Every component of the rover was designed in-house by UB ASME members, pushing the limits of additive manufacturing in structural applications.

Competed at Florida EFX, University of Central Florida (UCF) — 2026. The team represented UB on a national stage, showcasing innovative rover design and fabrication.
