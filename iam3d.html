2026 Rover — Florida EFX

Our 2026 rover was designed and built entirely using 3D printing — every single component, including the bearings, was printed in-house by the team. No off-the-shelf parts. The team traveled to Orlando, Florida to compete at Florida EFX at the University of Central Florida (UCF), representing UB ASME on a national stage.
